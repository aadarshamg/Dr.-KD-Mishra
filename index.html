What We Treat

Our Dermatology Services

Comprehensive skin and hair care treatments by Dr. K.D. Mishra — the best skin doctor in Bhagalpur. Each service is backed by clinical expertise and personalized care.

Service 01

Acne & Pimple Treatment

Acne is one of the most common skin conditions, affecting teenagers and adults alike. Dr. K.D. Mishra provides comprehensive acne treatment that targets the root cause — whether it's hormonal imbalance, bacterial infection, clogged pores, or excess oil production.

  • Reduction in active acne breakouts and inflammation
  • Treatment of blackheads, whiteheads, and cystic acne
  • Acne scar reduction and skin texture improvement
  • Personalized skincare routine guidance
  • Prevention of future breakouts with tailored treatment
Professional consultation is crucial because over-the-counter products often mask acne temporarily without treating the underlying cause. Dr. Mishra identifies the root cause for lasting results.
Service 02

Hair Fall & Hair Loss Treatment

Hair loss can be distressing and is often caused by nutritional deficiencies, hormonal changes, scalp conditions, genetic factors, or stress. Dr. Mishra conducts a thorough trichological assessment to identify the exact cause and create a targeted treatment plan.

  • Diagnosis of androgenetic alopecia, alopecia areata & more
  • Medical treatments to control and reverse hair loss
  • Scalp health improvement and dandruff management
  • Nutritional guidance for stronger, healthier hair
  • PRP therapy guidance and referral when needed
Early professional intervention significantly improves outcomes for hair loss. Self-treatment often delays effective care. Dr. Mishra's targeted approach gives the best chance of hair regrowth.
Service 03

Pigmentation & Dark Spots Treatment

Pigmentation issues such as melasma, sun spots, post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, and freckles can affect confidence. Dr. Mishra offers safe and effective treatments to even out skin tone and restore your skin's natural radiance.

  • Treatment of melasma, sun damage & post-acne dark spots
  • Prescription-grade skin lightening therapies
  • Sun protection protocol and skin barrier repair
  • Chemical peels guidance and medical-grade creams
  • Long-term pigmentation management plan
Pigmentation disorders require precise diagnosis as different types need different treatments. Using wrong products can worsen the condition — professional guidance is essential.
Service 04

Skin Allergy Treatment

Skin allergies manifest in many ways — redness, itching, rashes, hives, or eczema. Dr. Mishra helps identify the specific allergen or trigger and provides targeted medical management to relieve symptoms and prevent recurrence.

  • Diagnosis of contact dermatitis, eczema & urticaria (hives)
  • Allergen identification and avoidance counseling
  • Medical management for acute and chronic allergies
  • Relief from itching, redness, and inflammation
  • Long-term skin barrier strengthening strategies
Untreated skin allergies can worsen significantly and affect quality of life. Professional diagnosis helps identify the exact trigger and prevent future reactions effectively.
Service 05

Dark Circle Treatment

Dark circles under the eyes can result from genetics, lack of sleep, allergies, nutritional deficiencies, or skin thinning with age. Dr. Mishra evaluates the underlying cause and recommends safe, effective treatment options.

  • Assessment of pigmentation-type vs vascular dark circles
  • Targeted topical treatment with prescription-grade products
  • Guidance on lifestyle changes to reduce dark circles
  • Nutritional advice and deficiency correction
  • Refreshed, youthful under-eye appearance
Dark circles have multiple causes and the treatment differs based on type. Professional evaluation ensures you get the right treatment rather than wasting money on ineffective over-the-counter products.
Service 06

General Skin Disorders

Beyond specialized conditions, Dr. Mishra also treats a wide spectrum of general skin disorders including fungal infections, psoriasis, vitiligo, warts, corns, seborrheic dermatitis, and more. No skin concern is too small or complex.

  • Treatment of fungal, bacterial & viral skin infections
  • Management of psoriasis, vitiligo & chronic conditions
  • Removal of warts, skin tags, and moles (medical)
  • Nail disorder diagnosis and treatment
  • Comprehensive skin health check and maintenance
Many skin conditions look similar but require completely different treatments. A dermatologist's accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective treatment — avoid self-medicating skin conditions.
